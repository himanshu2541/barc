About Us

Branding and Relations cell is a student body under the aegis of the Office of Alumni Affairs and International Relations. It aims towards positioning IIT Kharagpur as a global leader in the field of science and technology.

WHAT WE DO

We support branding initiatives of

IIT KHARAGPUR

Young Innovators’ Program

Young Innovators’ Program is a platform to foster young minds with scientific enthusiasm and a credo to solve the problems facing the globe. IIT Kharagpur with a motive of recognizing students with creative ability to conduct scientific research has for the first time launched this program.

Social Media Outreach

Strengthening the IIT Kharagpur brand globally and communicate the motto ‘Dedicated to the service of the nation’ to the relevant audience, which includes IIT aspirants and students, academicians in India and abroad, the media that shapes the perspective, ranking institutions and governments.

Promotional Articles in the Media

Published various articles to add value to several branding avenues of the country, such as creation of customized content to enhance the reputation of the Institute through the digital and print media.

Foundation Day School Outreach Program

Y160 students from 8 schools participated in the IIT Kharagpur Foundation Day School Outreach Program organized by Office of Alumni Affairs and International Relations and Branding And Relations Cell, IIT Kharagpur.

YIP Publications

Presented Young Innovators Program as Coffee table book to President of India which got huge appreciation from President of India office

JEE Aspirants' Forum

An online platform for the students aspiring to join various engineering colleges which saw a participation of more than 13,000 students from all over India.
